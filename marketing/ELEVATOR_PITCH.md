## Objection Responses

**"Why not just use ChatGPT/Claude directly?"**
"Great question. ChatGPT and Claude are amazing, but they're general-purpose. CR AudioViz AI provides specialized tools built for specific creative workflows—document builders, graphic generators, business automation—all integrated with AI but optimized for output, not conversation."

**"What's your competitive moat?"**
"Three things: First, integration—everything works together instead of separately. Second, customer-first policies that no competitor matches—credits that never expire, automatic error refunds. Third, social impact focus—we're pursuing grants and building modules for communities others ignore."

**"How are you funded?"**
"We're self-funded and revenue-focused from day one. No VC pressure to grow at all costs. We're building sustainably with diversified revenue: subscriptions, marketplace, white-label, grants, and affiliates."

**"Who's your target customer?"**
"Three segments: Solo creators who need professional tools without enterprise costs. Small businesses automating their creative workflows. And enterprises wanting white-label solutions. We serve all three with different pricing tiers."
